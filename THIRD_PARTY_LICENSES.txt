--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
LICENSING NOTE — pyautogui removed (GPLv3 avoidance)
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Earlier versions used `pyautogui` (BSD-3-Clause) for text injection. It was
REMOVED because, as a hard dependency, it pulled in `mouseinfo` under the GPLv3
license (strong copyleft, incompatible with permissive MIT distribution). Its
functions (CTRL+V, typing) are now handled by `keyboard` (MIT), already present
in the project. The project intentionally contains NO GPL/AGPL dependencies.
